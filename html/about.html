What We Do

Throughout our 50-year history, we have adapted and grown with the auction industry, introducing new marketplaces, new technology and new tools to help auction houses grow their businesses, whilst providing new ways for bidders to access auctions across the world.
For bidders, we provide access to a curated selection of millions of unique and specialised secondary items in a trusted, simple, sustainable and convenient way.

Our mission is to be the global auction marketplace provider connecting bidders, businesses, collectors, contractors, dealers and consumers to an under-explored world of items curated by thousands of trusted auctioneer experts.

We aim to lead the evolution of the online auction industry from offline to online in two ways: by being the trusted partner to auctioneers; whilst simultaneously opening up the world of auctions to trade buyers and consumers alike.

We exist to unlock the value in the second, third and infinite reuse of the worldâ€™s items, from the every-day to the high value. All through auctions curated by trusted experts; and all for the benefit of auctioneers, their consignors, buyers and our planet. By giving millions of items multiple lives, we are accelerating the growth.

For Bidders

For bidders, ATG provides access to the widest array of specialised and unique items in the world offered in a curated online auction buying experience.

We invest heavily in marketing activities to bring new bidders to the world of auctions, and to make sure that existing bidders don't miss out on their favourite lots.

And we continually invest in our technology to ensure that we offer a bidder experience that is simple, secure, convenient, and enjoyable to use. So that bidders return time and time again.
